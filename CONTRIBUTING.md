# Contributing to Kubit

Thanks for your interest! Kubit is very new and evolving quickly.

- ⭐ Best way to support: star the repo and share it.
- 🐛 Found a bug? Open an Issue on GitHub.
- 💡 Have ideas? Open an Issue—feedback is most helpful right now.
- 🔧 PRs: small fixes (docs/typos/clear bugs) welcome; larger changes are on hold until things stabilize.
